Yannick CARLINET

Researcher at Orange, Innovation Division

Dr. Yannick CARLINET received his Ph.D. in computer science from University of Rennes, France in 2010. He also received an engineering degree from the "Ecole Nationale Supérieure des Mines de Nancy" in 1999. He is a former guest researcher at NIST (in the Advanced Network Technologies Division), in the Washington DC area, in 2000-2001. Currently, he is a researcher at Orange since 2002 and his current topics of interest include Edge Computing, network performance modeling and Network Virtualization, including Service Function Chaining and Slice Management.
